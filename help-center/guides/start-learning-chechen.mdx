## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up on Mott">
    Go to [mott.che](https://mott.che) and create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the basics module">
    Choose "Basics" from the dashboard to access beginner lessons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice pronunciation">
    Listen and repeat after recordings provided in each lesson.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your progress">
    Points and streaks let you see your improvement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
