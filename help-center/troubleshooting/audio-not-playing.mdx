---
title: Audio Not Playing
description: Resolve audio playback issues in lessons.
---

## Symptoms

- No sound in Chechen lessons
- Audio button not responding

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your volume">
    Ensure device sound is on and not muted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser permissions">
    Allow audio playback in your browser settings for mott.che.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another browser">
    Some browsers may block audio by default, so switch to Chrome or Firefox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
