## Overview

Mott empowers you to master the Chechen language with engaging resources designed to preserve and promote this rich cultural heritage. You access interactive lessons, quizzes, and community forums that make learning accessible and fun. Whether you seek basic phrases for travel or advanced fluency for deeper cultural exchange, Mott guides your journey from novice to proficient speaker.

<Callout kind="info">
Mott's mission centers on cultural preservation. You contribute to sustaining Chechen traditions while building global connections.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Begin your Chechen journey in minutes. Follow these steps to set up and complete your first lesson.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.mott.com` and sign up with your email. Verify your account to unlock free starter lessons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Lesson" icon="book-open">
    Choose "Beginner Basics" from the dashboard. Listen to native audio for phrases like "Соьга хьо" (Thank you).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take a Quiz" icon="check-circle">
    Complete the 5-question quiz. Review explanations for any mistakes to reinforce learning.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Community" icon="users">
    Post your first message in the forums: "Даймохк ду хьайн хуьлуь" (I am learning Chechen).
  </Step>
</Steps>
