## Prerequisites

Before you start, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- About 10 minutes of time
</Callout>

No prior Chechen knowledge is required—Mott guides you from beginner basics.

## Create Your Free Account

Sign up quickly to access personalized learning paths.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Mott" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and go to `https://app.mott.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select your learning goals (e.g., conversational Chechen).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from `no-reply@mott.com` and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Profile" icon="settings">
    Add your name, native language, and daily study time preference to tailor your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Beginner's Dashboard

Once logged in at `https://dashboard.mott.com`, you land on the beginner's dashboard. It shows your progress streak, daily goals, and recommended lessons.

## Complete Your First Interactive Lesson

Dive right into Chechen with a guided interactive session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Beginner Lesson" icon="play-circle">
    Click **"Start My First Lesson"** on the dashboard. Choose "Greetings and Introductions".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Interactive Prompts" icon="book-open">
    Listen to native pronunciations, repeat phrases like `Маршалла ду хьоьга` (Hello, how are you?), and complete quizzes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Track" icon="check-circle">
    Earn your first badge and see instant feedback. Save notes on new vocabulary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Lessons adapt to your pace—slow down for pronunciation practice or speed up if you know basics.
</Callout>
